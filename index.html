VC Vaccination Eligibility Checker (unofficial prototype)

Not an NHS service. This is an independent portfolio prototype created by a UX/service designer to demonstrate design and front-end skills. It is not produced, reviewed, endorsed, or affiliated with the NHS in any way.

Vaccine information referenced is based on the public guidance at nhs.uk, checked July 2026 — always confirm with a GP, pharmacist or midwife rather than relying on this tool.

Layout patterns are inspired by the open-source gov.uk / NHS.UK design system component libraries, used here for demonstration purposes only.
